Day 2 Keynote | Strategic AI Implementation Framework

+

üö® Industry Crisis Point

Game development budgets now reach $200M+ (vs $10-20M for Halo franchise), while competing with free entertainment on social platforms consuming 2-3 hours daily per user.

Critical Strategic Shifts:

Timeline Compression: AI moves so fast that traditional 3-horizon planning (immediate, 2-5 years, 5+ years) must collapse into single horizon thinking - plan in weeks and months, not years.

Human-Centered Implementation:

  • Wrong Approach: "Can AI replace your job?" (Answer: Always no)
  • Right Approach: "What technology would make your job easier?"
  • Cultural Challenge: Gaming creators want control over every pixel (wine making) vs AI requiring curation approach (wine tasting)

‚öñÔ∏è Jevons' Paradox in Action

Healthcare parallel: Despite predictions that AI would eliminate radiologists, demand actually increased. More efficient tools led to more applications, not fewer jobs.

Future Vision & Efficiency Goals:

  • 10 people creating AA games (vs current hundreds)
  • 50 people creating AAA titles (vs current 500-1000)
  • Focus on meaningful automation of tedious tasks
  • Challenge everything from first principles with AI mindset
Ethical Framework: Maintain Sony's ethical standards while embracing AI. "If you don't know if it's the right thing, ask someone." Human talent must remain at the center of all AI initiatives.

üéØ Recommended Actions

  • Immediate (Next 30 days): Identify specific workflow pain points where AI tools can provide immediate efficiency gains
  • Strategic Partnership: Evaluate external AI capabilities vs internal development for non-core competencies
  • Team Engagement: Implement human-centered AI adoption approach - focus on "making jobs easier" not replacement
  • Compressed Planning: Shift from annual/multi-year AI roadmaps to quarterly sprint-based evaluation cycles
  • Ethical Framework: Establish clear AI governance guidelines that maintain our integrity standards
  • Pilot Programs: Start small-scale AI integration projects with willing teams to demonstrate value before broader rollout
